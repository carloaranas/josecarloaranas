Chadwick Boseman

The Real Black Panther

November 29, 1976 - August 28, 2020
Early Life

Chadwick Aaron Boseman was born on November 29th, 1976 in Anderson, South Carolina. His mother was a nurse and his father worked at a textile factory while managing his own upholstery business. He graduated from T. L. Hanna High School in 1995, Howard University in 2000 with a Bachelor of Arts in Directing, and went on to graduate school from the Digital Film Academy located in New York City. In 2008, he moved to Los Angeles, California to pursue his acting career.

Personal Life

Boseman was raised as a Christian and was baptized at an early age. He began dating Taylor Simone Ledward in 2015 and was engaged to her in October of 2019. They then married in secret according to Chadwick Boseman's family during the announcement of his death.

Reason of Death

Boseman was diagnosed with Stage 3 Colon Cancer in 2016. The cancer progressed to Stage 4 sometime before 2020. Chadwick never spoke about his cancer diagnosis publicly and only a handful of non-family members knew that he was sick. He underwent multiple surgeries and chemotherapy while continuing his acting career. On August 28th, 2020 Boseman died at 43 years old in his home as a result of complications related to Colon Cancer. He had his wife and family by his side. A public memorial service was held on September 4th, 2020 in Anderson, South Carolina. Boseman was buried at Welfare Baptist Church cemetery in the nearby community of Belton, South Carolina.
