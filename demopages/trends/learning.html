ClassVR

ClassVR is a classroom focused product which implements Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) systems. It enables the users to experience any learning environment they need with just an AR or VR headset. This system also contains educational content that is curriculum-aligned.

ClassVR is one of the successful products made by Avantis Systems. It is a leading educational technology company which have developed many technological ideas to provide modern solutions to modern educational problems.

Beneficiaries:

This technology is beneficial to students of any educational level for it can help them stay focused and concentrated in the interaction with their studies. It also helps them in making their lectures and sessions more immersive and interactive, making it a good solution to retain students' focus and attention. Moreover, this system can also help in eliminating language barriers, for it has a language translation feature, which can really help students, especially those who grew up knowing only foreign languages.
