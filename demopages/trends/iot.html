Microsoft Virtual Stage

Virtual Stage is a virtual green screen which uses a background matting process from the approach of Adobe Research and University of Washington, and the Azure Kinect sensors of Windows. This project enables users to create high-quality videos from anywhere.

Virtual Stage is a project developed by Windows with the help of the approach of Adobe Research and University of Washington on background matting for video. Windows is an American multinational company which produces technologies such as computer software, personal computers, consumer electronics, and other related products and services.

Beneficiaries:

This technology is beneficial to everyone, especially those people who are working or studying at home. This technology enabled immersive, AI-powered presentations, which improved video content and will keep us better engaged with our coworkers.
